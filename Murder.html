My Recommendations

"An Unwanted Guest" Shari Lapena

Stuck at a hotel because of a blizzard, no power, and no way to contact the ourside world things can get deadly. Especially, when one guest ends up murder. Find out who, if any, can survive a blizzard and a murderer.

"And Then There Were None" Agatha Christie

Ten people are invited to remote island, by an unknown guest. Everything seems normal until the host doesn't arrive, but rather leaves the guest with a nursery rhyme. The guest need to work together to figure out who their mystery host is before there is no one left to wonder.

Best Sellers

"The Alienist" Caleb Carr

Set in 1896 New York, follow Dr. Kreizler and his crew, as they try to piece together who is this killer that is murdering these male child prostitutes.

"The Da Vinci Code" Dan Brown

The Catholic Church is a very large and powerful organization and religion. It will take one man using the clues left by Leonardo DaVinci to solve the mystery that the church has been trying to keep hidden.
